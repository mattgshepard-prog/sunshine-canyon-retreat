Mountain travel and outdoor risk

The Property is a private mountain home in Boulder County, Colorado, at elevation. By booking, you acknowledge and accept the ordinary risks associated with mountain travel, including:

  • Wildlife encounters (bears, deer, mountain lions, rodents, insects).
  • Weather events affecting road access (snow, ice, mud, washouts, downed trees).
  • Wildfire risk, smoke, and reduced air quality during fire season.
  • High-altitude effects such as dehydration, shortness of breath, and altitude sickness.
  • Uneven terrain, stairs, decks, and outdoor structures.

You and your group use the Property and the surrounding area at your own risk. We strongly recommend travel insurance for every trip.
