The Retreat

Where the canyon meets the sky

Tucked into the foothills west of Boulder along historic Sunshine Canyon Drive, this mountain retreat sits at the intersection of wilderness and civilization. The property offers approximately 4,100 square feet of living space with five bedrooms and four bathrooms — enough room for family reunions, creative retreats, or simply spreading out and breathing deep.

Sunshine Canyon earned its name honestly. The south-facing ridgeline captures more sunlight than the surrounding valleys, and the property takes full advantage — panoramic views of the Continental Divide, a hot tub perched among the pines, and the kind of silence you forgot existed.

This is not a hotel. It is a home — one that happens to sit in one of Colorado's most spectacular locations.

Explore the Area

Boulder at your doorstep,
wilderness at your back

15 minutes

Pearl Street & Downtown Boulder

World-class dining, craft breweries, independent shops, and the pedestrian Pearl Street Mall. Farm-to-table restaurants like SALT, morning pastries at Spruce Confections, and the vibrant energy of a true mountain town.

At your door

Sunshine Canyon Trail System

Step out the door and into Boulder's trail network. The Sunshine Canyon Trail connects to Mount Sanitas, Red Rocks Spur, and the Centennial Trailhead. Hiking, trail running, mountain biking — all world-class, all minutes away.

10 minutes

Gold Hill

Colorado's first major gold discovery site. This tiny mountain hamlet features the Gold Hill General Store & Pub for homecooked meals and craft beer, and the Gold Hill Inn's legendary beer garden with live music under the pines.

20 minutes

Chautauqua Park & the Flatirons

Boulder's crown jewel. Hike beneath the iconic Flatiron rock formations, explore the historic Chautauqua grounds, or just stare up at one of Colorado's most photographed landscapes.
